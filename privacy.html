UtterText

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

NO DATA COLLECTION

UtterText does not collect, transmit, or store any user data. All voice processing happens locally on your device using the open-source Whisper AI model.

WHAT WE DON'T DO

  • We don't record or store your voice
  • We don't send data to any servers
  • We don't track your usage
  • We don't sell or share any information
  • We don't require an account

LOCAL PROCESSING

All transcriptions are processed entirely on your Mac. Audio is temporarily stored in memory during recording and immediately deleted after transcription. No audio files are saved to disk.

IN-APP PURCHASES

Purchase information is handled exclusively by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase transactions.

MICROPHONE ACCESS

UtterText requires microphone access solely for voice recording. Audio is processed locally and never transmitted to any external servers. You can revoke microphone access at any time in System Settings.

APPLE EVENTS ACCESS

UtterText can request accessibility permissions to automatically paste transcribed text into other applications. This is optional and only used for the auto-paste functionality. You can use the app in clipboard-only mode if you prefer not to grant this permission.

NETWORK ACCESS

The app only uses network access for two purposes:

  • Downloading AI models from Hugging Face (one-time, optional downloads)
  • Verifying in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit servers

No user data, voice recordings, or transcriptions are ever transmitted over the network.

ACCURACY DISCLAIMER & LIMITATIONS OF LIABILITY

UtterText uses AI-powered speech recognition technology which, while advanced, may produce inaccurate or incomplete transcriptions. The accuracy of transcriptions can vary based on audio quality, accents, background noise, technical terms, and other factors.

IMPORTANT: UtterText is provided "as is" for general productivity purposes only. It is NOT intended for:

  • Legal proceedings, depositions, or court records
  • Medical transcription or patient records
  • Safety-critical applications
  • Financial or contractual documents
  • Any application where transcription errors could result in harm, legal liability, or financial loss

Users are solely responsible for verifying the accuracy of all transcriptions. By using UtterText, you acknowledge that:

  • Transcriptions may contain errors or inaccuracies
  • You will review and verify all transcribed content before use
  • The developer is not liable for any damages, losses, or consequences arising from inaccurate transcriptions
  • You will not use the app for applications requiring certified accuracy

Always proofread transcriptions before using them in any important context.

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

YOUR RIGHTS

Since UtterText collects no personal data:

  • There is no data to request, modify, or delete
  • You can uninstall the app at any time with no data remaining
  • Your privacy is absolute and complete
