Frequently Asked Questions

How does UtterText protect my privacy?

All voice processing happens entirely on your Mac using the open-source Whisper AI model. Your voice is never uploaded to any servers. We don't collect, store, or transmit any user data.

What languages are supported?

UtterText supports 99+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, and many more. Language is detected automatically.

Do I need an internet connection?

Internet is only required once to download the AI model (75MB-1.5GB depending on quality). After that, all transcription works completely offline.

How accurate are the transcriptions?

UtterText uses state-of-the-art Whisper AI technology. Accuracy depends on audio quality, background noise, and accent. We recommend reviewing transcriptions before use in important contexts.

What's the difference between free and premium?

Free tier: 10 trial transcriptions, then 3 per day forever. Premium: Unlimited transcriptions. Available as monthly ($2.99/mo), yearly ($9.99/yr), or lifetime ($19.99 one-time — best value).
